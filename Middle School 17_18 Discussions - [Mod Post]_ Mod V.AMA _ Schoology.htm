MERRY CHRISTMAS FROM LORD POTATO & THE MSD MODS

( You're welcome for the red and green Christmas colors )

How does it work?

Each mod will post a comment if s/he wishes to participate in the exchange. I will also post a comment for you to ask a questions for all of us to answer. You will respond to our comments with your questions, but please do not post a top-level comment. If you do, it will be deleted. We'll answer as many questions as we can.

Please don't ask any of us more than two questions.

When is the deadline?

I'm so glad you asked! Someone put their thinking cap on this morning.

The deadline is Wednesday, 12/20 at 11:55 PM EST. Once the deadline is reached, I'll save the comments and delete the discussion.

What ever happened to last year's video?

The mod responsible for the video did not produce the video. I released my footage, as did many other mods. However, the main video was never compiled. I'm sorry for the disappointment, but it's not any of our fault. 

You're not going to do that again, are you?

Heck no.

What about a Secret Santa gift exchange, like HSD has?

If you have friends in HSD, you may have heard about the Secret Santa exchange. I've decided not to run one for MSD because 1) I don't think there are as many people in this group who have the means to buy and ship a gift; 2) in light of some recent issues, I don't think it would be wise to actively encourage you to hand out your addresses; 3) I foresee many parents being unhappy with the concept.

TL;DR

Respond to our comments with three or fewer questions, and we'll answer them in a video. Do it before Wednesday the 20th.
